Delete your ColorCollect data

Last updated: 2026-05-25

This page explains how to request deletion of the data that the ColorCollect app (published by Lancimator) holds about you, what gets deleted, and what — if anything — is kept.

For the full picture of what the app collects in the first place, see our Privacy Policy.

Option 1 — Delete everything from inside the app (recommended)

The fastest way is to use the built-in self-service flow. It works even if you have never created an account in the traditional sense (ColorCollect uses an anonymous account id that is generated automatically the first time you open Group mode).

  1. Open the ColorCollect app on your device.
  2. Tap the Settings icon in the top-right of the home screen.
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap "Delete account & all data".
  4. Confirm in the dialog that appears.

This wipes every montage, cell photo, room, setting, and the anonymous account id on your device, and asks our backend (Supabase) to delete every server-side row tied to your anonymous account — participations, photo uploads, votes, abuse-prevention counters, and the authentication record itself.

Note: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. After it finishes, the app returns to its first-launch state and you will be issued a brand-new anonymous account id the next time you open Group mode.

Option 2 — Email us

If the in-app delete fails (e.g. you are offline, the app no longer launches, or you have already uninstalled it), send a deletion request by email:

  • To: lancimator@gmail.com
  • Subject: ColorCollect data deletion request
  • Include: the anonymous account id shown in Settings → About, if you still have access to it. If you don't, list any room codes you participated in and a rough date range so we can locate the records.

We will confirm receipt within 7 days and complete the deletion within 30 days.

What is kept, and for how long

A small amount of data is retained briefly for legal, security, and operational reasons:

DataWhy we keep itHow long
Backend access logs (including the IP address your device connected from) Security, abuse detection, and rate limiting. Logged by Supabase under their own policy; we do not separately store or analyse these. Up to 30 days, then deleted by Supabase.
Crash diagnostics (only if you opted into crash reporting) Stack traces uploaded to Sentry to help us fix bugs. These contain no photos, no display names, and no device identifiers — only an anonymous diagnostic id. Up to 90 days at Sentry, then deleted.
Email correspondence about your deletion request (Option 2 only) So we can prove the request was honoured if you ask later. Up to 12 months, then deleted.
Group-room photos that other participants shared into rooms you joined Those photos belong to the other participants and remain visible to the rest of the room. We only delete photos you uploaded. Auto-deleted from our servers shortly after the room is finalized (typically within an hour), and at most 24 hours after a room goes inactive, per our normal retention policy.

Uninstalling the app

Uninstalling ColorCollect from your device removes all local data (montages, photos, settings, geotags). It does not by itself remove anything from our backend — for that you must use Option 1 before uninstalling, or Option 2 afterwards.
